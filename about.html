about us

Our Story

Beauty with Purpose

Founded in 2010, Karma Beauty Salon began as a small neighborhood salon with a big vision: to create a space where beauty meets wellness, and where every client feels valued and transformed.

Our founder, Elena Rodriguez, started with just two styling chairs and a dream to provide exceptional beauty services in a welcoming environment. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service salon and spa, but our core values remain the same.

We believe that true beauty comes from confidence and self-care. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to helping you look and feel your best, using only the highest quality products and the latest techniques.

At Karma, we're more than just a salon - we're a community. We've built lasting relationships with our clients and take pride in being part of their beauty journeys.
